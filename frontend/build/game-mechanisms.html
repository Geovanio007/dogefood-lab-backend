DogeFood Lab

Complete Game Mechanics Guide

🎮Game Overview

DogeFood Lab is a Web3-powered treat creation game where you become a scientist mixing cosmic ingredients to craft unique treats. Compete on leaderboards, level up, discover secret recipes, and prepare for $LAB token rewards!

Core Objectives

  • Create Treats: Mix 1-5 ingredients to craft treats with varying rarities
  • Level Up: Gain XP to unlock new ingredient categories
  • Compete: Climb the leaderboard by earning points
  • Discover: Find secret recipes for bonus rewards
  • Collect: Build your collection of rare and Mythic treats

✨Rarity System

Your treat's rarity determines its value, points, and XP rewards. Use more ingredients and higher-tier components to increase your chances!

Rarity Min Ingredients Points XP Requirements
Common 1 10-20 5-10 Any ingredient
Uncommon 2 25-40 15-25 2+ ingredients
Rare 3 50-80 30-50 3+ ingredients
Epic 4 100-150 60-100 4+ ingredients
Legendary 5 200-300 120-200 5 ingredients + Legendary Gate
Mythic 5 500-1000 250-500 5 ingredients + Mythic ingredient

Rarity Boosting Tips

  • Legendary Gate: Use Starship Alloy, Mars Regolith, or Dark Matter to unlock Legendary potential
  • Mythic Gate: Zero-G Gel unlocks Mythic potential when combined with Mythic ingredients
  • Rarity Weight: Each ingredient has a rarity weight - higher weights boost your chances
